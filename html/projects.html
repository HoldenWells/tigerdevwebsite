Gaia
Team Leaders : Keola Silva and Michelle Brannan

Team Name : Team Gaia

Description : Gaia is a 2D side-scrolling platforming game in a 3D world inspired by Paper Mario. You play as Gaia, a creature capable of transforming into different forms that give him different abilities. Gaia can gain the ability to transform into other forms once he collects enough crystal shards of each type: green shards to become the forest ranger, red shards to become the desert wanderer, and blue shards to become the arctic bear. Your goal is to help Gaia add these abilities to his arsenal so he can defeat the final boss, Fenrir.
